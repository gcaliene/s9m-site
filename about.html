About S9M

what is sharp 9 media

Websites for musicians, by musicians

Sharp 9 Media was created with the working musician in mind. Let's face it, musicians have their hands full. There's all the work involved with building a fan base, song writing, looking for and booking gigs, keeping track of any merchandise... oh and let's not forget that ever sought after practice time!

We at Sharp 9 Media understand these many distractions. Why? Because we are all either current or former performing musicians.

Free up some valuable time and save yourself some stress. Sharp 9 Media can set you up with an afordable and modern website that can serve as a centralized place where music fans can find out about you and your music.
